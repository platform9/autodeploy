------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Configuration Instructions
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Prior to running Auto-Deploy, you must configure your environemnt.

Step 1 : Configure Ansile Inventory
- Edit your 'inventory/hosts file' with the hostnames and IP addresses of the hypervisors
  you want to assimilate into your Platform9 control plane.

- Here is a sample 'inventry/hosts' for reference:
  [hypervisors]
  ilab_kvm_host_0 ansible_host=172.16.7.48
  ilab_kvm_host_1 ansible_host=172.16.7.49

  [hypervisors:vars]
  ansible_user=centos
  ansible_ssh_pass=Pl@tform9
  ansible_sudo_pass=Pl@tform9

Step 2 : Configure Platform9 Control Plane
- Edit your 'group_vars/all.yml' file with the URL and credentials for your Platform9 control plane.

- Here is a sample 'group_vars/all.yml' for reference:
  os_username: admin@platform9.net
  os_password: o7tsH7UZEVoCIswx
  os_region: master
  os_tenant: service
  du_url: https://danwright.platform9.horse/
